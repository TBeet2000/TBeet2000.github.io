About me

Welcome to my website! My name is Thomas and I am a second year PhD student from Royal Holloway in the department of mathematics.

My research area lies within quantum computation and quantum information theory.

I got my masters degree in physics at the University of Lincoln in 2022 and in my spare time I like to play squash, cycle and play historical strategy games.

Research Areas

My active research interests are in the following:

  • Quantum State Transfer
  • Quantum Communication
  • Realising physical quantum computers

My other research interests include:

  • Quantum models of cognition
  • Quantum thermodynamics/ statistical mechanics
